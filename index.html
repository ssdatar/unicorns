Asian startups fast on US' heels

World's Most Valuable Companies

"Unicorn" companies are private companies valued at more than $1 billion. By 2015, the world had 150 such firms.

Bigger than countries

While the U.S. has most of them, Asia, too, is thriving. The total valuation of Asian unicorns was more than the GDP of countries like New Zealand and Oman.

Where are they?

China and India together account for one-third of the unicorns. Israel and Singapore have two each. Thailand has one.

The Top Ten

Industries

eCommerce is the most common unicorns in Asia. Hardware and on-demand services (cabs, for instance) are next.
